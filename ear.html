Accomplishmnets Early Life

Musk was born on the 28th June 1971 Pretoria, South Africa. As a child musk developed an interest in computing and computer programming, and along with his brother Kimbal they developed a video-game called Blastar which they later sold to “PC and Office Technology” magazine, for approximately $500.

Musk was accepted into Queen’s University at the age of 17, here he studied for two years before transferring to University of Pennsylvania where Musk later received his Bachelor of Science degrees in Physics and Economics. Musk Later moved to California to begin studies for his PhD at Stanford University, however Musk left the program after only two days to start his entrepreneurial endeavours.
